HOW TO ADD YOUR PROFILE PHOTO:
================================
1. Save your photo as: photo.jpg
2. Put it in this /public/ folder
3. Open components/Hero.tsx
4. Find the line:  {/* <img src="/photo.jpg" ... */}
5. Remove the {/* and */} comment tags
6. Also remove or comment the emoji placeholder div above it
7. Save and refresh — your photo will appear in the circle!

Recommended photo size: 400x400px (square)
